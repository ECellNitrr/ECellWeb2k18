Startup camp 2018

2 startups( Govatsa and exosolar) were selected for investment under a nonclosure deal.

Exosolar Pvt Ltd is the only India based company that develops unique styled robots to clean solar panels quickly and with greater efficiency. Exosolar has developed dual natured robot that can give wet as well as dry cleaning thus reducing the consumption of water and boosting up generation of electricity.

GoVatsa makes Bioproducts for agricultural work ,Unique organic plant extract that induce the plant to self-produce higher levels of natural occurring hormones, which improves plant health, vigour and end yields.

Ignition is the business model competition designed to emulate the process of the growth of an idea towards a full-fledged startup. The basic motive of this B-model is to connect the budding entrepreneurs with investors giving start-ups a leap. Right from acknowledging that your idea has potential, to drafting a B-Model, and pitching in front of an esteemed panel of investor- this platform has it all.
